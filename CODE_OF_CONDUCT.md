# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, professional, and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical outcomes and evidence.
- Accept feedback in good faith.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing, intimidation, or threats.
- Deliberate disruption of project processes.
- Publishing private/security-sensitive information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation for conduct violations.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues to `hello@neuraminds.io`.

All reports are reviewed confidentially.
